What is Twitter?

Introduction to Twitter

Twitter is a popular social media platform that allows users to send and read short messages called "tweets." These messages are limited to 280 characters and can include links, images, videos, and hashtags. Twitter has become a vital platform for news, entertainment, and social interaction worldwide.

Key Features of Twitter

  • Tweets: Users can post short updates, called tweets, to share thoughts, ideas, or information.
  • Followers: Users can follow other accounts to receive their tweets on their feed.
  • Trending Topics: Twitter highlights the most talked-about topics, making it easy to stay updated.
  • Retweets: Users can share other people's tweets to their own followers.
  • Hashtags: Hashtags help group related tweets together, making it easier to find specific topics or events.

Twitter is used by individuals, celebrities, organizations, and companies to engage with their audience, share opinions, and keep up with what's happening in real-time. It has a major influence on social movements, politics, and pop culture.
